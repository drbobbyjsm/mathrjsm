About Movember

Prostate cancer, testicular cancer, and suicide

Globally almost 10 million men are living with or beyond prostate cancer. It is estimated that 8,000 to 10,000 men will develop testicular cancer each year. The chance of developing testis cancer is about one in 270, and of those, about 1 in 20 die. In the united states, On average, there are 123 suicides per day, with men dying by suicide 3.5 times more often than women.

Men aren't getting help

For many reasons, pride and masculinity among them, men aren't getting the regular checkups, screenings, and preventative care they need.

Movember, and what I'm doing to help

Throughout the month of November (and possibly longer depending on how much is raised), I will be growing a ridiculous handlebar mustache and mutton chops to spark conversation and raise awareness about men's health, and support the Movember Foundation through donations that I can raise. Movember seeks to raise awareness, and raise money for men's health through cancer research, prevention, and early intervention with mental illness.

"We work closely with our global men's health partners to ensure collaboration, transparency and accountability for every project we fund. We monitor this through report cards which detail what we seek to achieve, key measures and the impact." - Owen Sharp, CEO
