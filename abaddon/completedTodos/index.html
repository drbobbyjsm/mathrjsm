Completed Todos Export

Use the form below to export the last thirty of your completed todos. Note that the information you will find here is the same as that in the Habitica Official User Data Display Tool at https://oldgods.net/habitrpg/habitrpg_user_data_display.html or in Settings >> Data Export on the Habitica web app at https://habitica.com/user/settings/data-export. The difference is that the data will be exported as a .csv (comma separated value)
